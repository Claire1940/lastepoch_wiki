## Key Gameplay Features to Explore

If you are picking up the game during this sale, here are the core systems you should prioritize learning:

*   **The Mastery System:** You start as one of five base classes (Mage, Rogue, Primalist, Acolyte, Sentinel). At roughly level 25, you commit to one of three Masteries. This choice is permanent, so research your playstyle beforehand!
*   **Deterministic Crafting:** Unlike the heavy RNG of other ARPGs, Last Epoch allows you to specifically add and upgrade the stats you want on your gear using Shards.
*   **Time Travel Narrative:** The campaign takes you through four distinct eras of the world of Eterra (Divine, Ruined, Imperial, and Ancient), with your actions in the past often reflecting in the future landscape.
