## Last Epoch Campaign Guide: Chapter Walkthrough

The campaign spans nine distinct chapters. While the story is linear, there are several "danger zones" and optional side quests that provide permanent character upgrades. In 2026, the most efficient path focuses on completing only the side quests that grant **Passive Points** or **Idol Slots**.

### Chapters 1-3: The Foundations
The early game focuses on the Divine and Ruined Eras. Your primary goal is to reach the Council Chambers and eventually the End of Time.

*   **Chapter 1:** Focus on getting to level 4 before the first boss, Haruspex Orian. Look for **Silver Rings** at vendors for early movement speed.
*   **Chapter 2:** Complete the "Evacuation" quest for an easy passive point. This chapter introduces the **Voidfused Forge** boss—stay out of the lava pools he creates.
*   **Chapter 3:** This chapter is heavy on Physical and Void damage. Ensure you complete the "Lesser Refuge" quest in the Shattered Valley for two vital passive points.

### Chapters 4-6: The Imperial Era
This segment increases the difficulty significantly, introducing Necrotic and Poison damage types.

*   **Chapter 4:** You will face Admiral Harton on his Dreadnought. Stay close to him to prevent his devastating lightning beam attacks.
*   **Chapter 5:** This is widely considered the "Hardest Transition." Upon landing on the beach, **stop and craft**. You need Physical and Poison resistance to survive the Nagasa enemies.
*   **Chapter 6:** Infiltrate the Immortal Citadel. You will fight a triple boss encounter (Yulia, Harton, and Zerrick). Focus on Yulia first as she can heal her allies.

### Chapters 7-9: The Divine Era and Majasa
The final stretch takes you through the frozen north and the deserts of Maj'Elka.

*   **Chapter 7:** Cold resistance is mandatory. The Frostroot Warden boss is made easier by following Grael's lead; he will periodically freeze the boss for you.
*   **Chapter 8:** The Lagon fight is a major "stat check." You must cap your Cold and Lightning resistances. Watch his eye—if it rolls to the side, a massive "Sweep" beam is coming.
*   **Chapter 9:** The final chapter features Majasa. This two-phase fight tests your Elemental resistance in Phase 1 and your Physical/Armor stats in Phase 2.

| **Chapter 7** | 2 Idol Slots | Cold / Physical |
| **Chapter 8** | 1 Passive Point | Lightning / Cold |
| **Chapter 9** | Passive + Attributes | All Elemental |

## Essential Leveling Mechanics

To maintain efficiency, you must utilize the game's built-in systems. Ignoring crafting or loot filters will result in a "gear wall" where enemies begin to one-shot your character.

### Crafting Early and Often
In Last Epoch, crafting is not an endgame-only activity. Pressing 'F' opens the Forge at any time. Use **Affix Shards** to add Health and Resistances to your gear as you find it. 
*   **Runes of Shattering:** Use these on items with high-tier affixes you need for your build.
*   **Glyphs of Hope:** Always use these when upgrading to preserve **Forging Potential**.

### Using the Loot Filter
By Chapter 2, you should have a basic loot filter active. Press `Shift + F` to open the menu. At a minimum, set a rule to **Hide** all Normal (White) and Magic (Blue) items. This prevents screen clutter and allows you to focus on Rare and Unique drops that actually improve your build.

> **Warning:** Chapter 5 (The Majasan Desert) features a massive spike in Poison damage. If your Poison resistance is below 40%, you will likely die to the first pack of Nagasa. Use the Forge to add Poison Res to your jewelry immediately.

## Boss Strategy Guide

Several bosses in the campaign act as gatekeepers. If you find yourself stuck, check your resistances against the table below and adjust your gear accordingly.

### Lagon, God of Storms (Chapter 8)
Lagon is a three-phase fight. You can only damage him by attacking his tentacles.
1.  **Phase 1:** Watch the eye. When it glows and moves, run to the opposite side of the arena to avoid the sweep.
2.  **Phase 2:** You are teleported below. Avoid the waves and kill tentacles quickly to return to the top.
3.  **Phase 3:** A repeat of Phase 1, but with increased frequency of Lightning bolts.

### Majasa, Goddess of Opulence (Chapter 9)
Majasa is the final campaign challenge and requires high mechanical awareness.
*   **Phase 1 (Elemental):** She uses Fire, Lightning, and Poison. When she says "This will be your tomb," she summons crystals. **Destroy the Fire crystal first**, as its damage is the most consistent.
*   **Phase 2 (Physical):** Majasa becomes a melee bruiser. She will shred your armor with every hit. You must kite her and use traversal skills to escape her "Blood Pulse" explosion.

## Speedrunning and Efficiency Tips

If you are looking to reach the [official Last Epoch endgame](https://lastepoch.com/) faster, incorporate these professional speedrunning tactics into your run:

1.  **The "Silver Ring" Strat:** Always check vendors in Chapter 1 for Silver Rings. Their implicit movement speed speeds up the entire campaign.
2.  **Skip Unnecessary Mobs:** You do not need to kill every enemy. Focus on large packs for XP and keep moving toward the quest marker.
3.  **TP Refreshing:** If a zone is a dead end after a quest objective, use a Town Portal (T) to return to town and use the Waypoint to reach the next area instantly.
4.  **Monolith Skip:** You can actually enter the Monolith of Fate as early as level 25 after reaching the End of Time. While it is difficult, farming the first few echoes can give you a massive level advantage for the later campaign chapters.

## Transitioning to the Endgame

Once Majasa falls, the campaign technically ends, but your character's journey is just beginning. You will be directed to the End of Time to begin the **Monolith of Fate**. At this stage, you should also choose your Item Faction in Maj'Elka:
*   **Circle of Fortune:** Best for players who want to find their own loot with massive drop rate buffs.
*   **Merchant’s Guild:** Best for players who prefer using the Bazaar to trade gold for specific "BiS" (Best in Slot) items.

## FAQ

**Q: Do I have to finish the whole campaign to start the endgame?**

A: No. You gain access to the Monolith of Fate after Chapter 2. However, it is highly recommended to play through Chapter 9 to unlock all 15 Passive Points, 8 Idol Slots, and the +1 to all attributes bonus.

**Q: What should I do if I keep dying in Chapter 5?**

A: Chapter 5 is a notorious difficulty spike. Check this **last epoch campaign guide** resistance table—you likely need more Poison and Physical resistance. Spend some time at the Forge adding "Health" and "Poison Resistance" affixes to your gear.

**Q: Can I change my Mastery later?**

A: No. While you can respec your skills and passives at any time for gold, your Mastery choice (e.g., Paladin vs. Void Knight) is permanent for that character. Choose wisely!

**Q: How do I unlock more Idol Slots?**

A: Idol slots are rewarded through specific side quests throughout the campaign. If you follow the main quest and complete the major side paths in Chapters 3, 4, 5, and 7, you will reach the maximum of 8 slots (20 grid spaces).
