## Reporting Bugs and Getting Technical Help

No game is without its technical hurdles, and Last Epoch provides a robust system for reporting issues. If you experience an error like "LE-52" or find that your chat window font size needs adjustment, the forum is the place to go.

1.  **Search First:** Use the search bar to see if your bug has already been reported. This prevents duplicate threads and helps EHG track the frequency of specific issues.
2.  **Provide Logs:** For crashes or performance drops, providing your player logs and system specs is vital.
3.  **Be Descriptive:** Instead of saying "the game broke," specify the skill used, the map you were in, and what happened immediately before the crash.

> **⚠️ Warning:** Never share your account password or sensitive personal information on public forum threads. Official EHG staff will never ask for your password.
