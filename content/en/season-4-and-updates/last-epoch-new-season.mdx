## Preparing for the Last Epoch New Season

To succeed in the **last epoch new season**, you must choose a challenge mode that fits your playstyle. The game offers several ways to experience the new content, ranging from casual play to hardcore competitive runs.

*   **Standard Season:** The most popular way to play, featuring a shared economy and multiplayer capabilities.
*   **Hardcore:** For those seeking the thrill of permanent death. If your character dies, they are moved to the Legacy realm.
*   **Solo Self-Found (SSF):** Ideal for players who prefer a "lone wolf" experience without trading or partying.
*   **Masochist Mode:** An unofficial but popular community challenge for those who find the base game too easy.

> **⚠️ Warning:** Seasonal characters cannot share items with Legacy characters. Ensure you are creating your character in the "Season" or "Cycle" tab to access the latest mechanics.
