## Mastery Classes and Skill Customization

A core reason for the game's longevity beyond the **last epoch release date** is its class system. Players choose one of five base classes—Mage, Primalist, Sentinel, Rogue, or Acolyte—and eventually ascend into one of three specialized Mastery Classes.

*   **15 Mastery Classes:** Each offers a completely different playstyle, from the void-consuming Void Knight to the minion-commanding Necromancer.
*   **120+ Skill Trees:** Every single active skill in the game has its own dedicated augment tree. This allows you to transform a simple fireball into a circling flame shield or a massive volcanic eruption.
*   **Deterministic Crafting:** Unlike the heavy RNG found in other ARPGs, this system allows you to specifically target the stats you want on your gear, making the hunt for "Best in Slot" items feel rewarding rather than frustrating.
