## Unlocking the Harbinger of Hatred

To encounter the Harbinger of Hatred, you must first progress through the standard endgame loop. Harbingers do not appear until you have reached the level 90 timelines. Specifically, the first Harbinger in your journey will spawn after you defeat the third boss of your final level 90 timeline. 

Once the initial Harbinger is slain, you gain access to the **Shattered Road**, the central hub for the Forgotten Knights faction. From this point forward, you can track your progress through the Harbinger sub-menu in your faction tab (default key: **Y**). 

The **last epoch harbinger of hatred** specifically resides within **The Black Sun** timeline. To trigger its appearance, you must meet the following criteria:

*   **Timeline:** Reach the Black Sun timeline (Empowered version recommended).
*   **Corruption:** Meet the minimum corruption threshold indicated in your Forgotten Knights progression window.
*   **Stability:** Farm enough Echo Stability to challenge the timeline boss, Rahyeh, The Black Sun.
*   **The Encounter:** After Rahyeh is defeated, the Harbinger of Hatred will immediately spawn in the arena, provided you have the Harbinger quest active and meet the corruption requirements.

## Boss Mechanics and Archetype

Every Harbinger in Last Epoch follows a specific combat archetype. The Harbinger of Hatred is classified as a **Tank Archetype**, meaning it possesses high health pools and heavy-hitting, telegraphed physical and void attacks. In addition to standard Tank skills, it inherits several mechanics from Rahyeh, the boss of the Black Sun.

### Key Abilities and Survival Strategies

| Ability Name | Damage Type | Mechanic Description | Survival Tip |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Void Slam** | **Void** | A large, circular AOE slam directly in front of the boss. | Move behind the boss as soon as the purple indicator appears. |
| **Echo Slam** | **Physical** | A ground slam that sends shockwaves outward from the impact point. | Avoid standing in the center; the shockwaves deal multi-hit damage. |
| **Void Flares** | **Void** | Orbs that rotate around the arena in a clockwise pattern. | Follow the rotation or use a traversal skill to blink through the gaps. |
| **Void Skyfall** | **Void** | Multiple projectiles rain down from above in targeted zones. | Keep moving constantly; do not stand still while the boss is channeling. |

> **⚠️ Warning:** Do not confuse this boss with the "Harbinger of Defilement." Some players have reported a naming bug where the boss appears with the wrong title in the arena. As long as you are in the Black Sun timeline, it will count as the Harbinger of Hatred for your faction progress.

## Rewards and Loot Table

Defeating the Harbinger of Hatred is the only reliable way to obtain certain endgame items. The loot quality scales significantly with corruption, making high-level farming a priority for players seeking Legendary Potential (LP).

### Exclusive Drops

*   **World Splitter:** A powerful unique Two-Handed Axe designed for Void-based melee builds. It is a highly sought-after item for Void Knights.
*   **Harbinger Eye:** A guaranteed drop upon your first kill for the questline. These eyes serve as keys to enter the arena of Aberroth, the Pinnacle Boss.
*   **Harbinger's Needle:** An idol-slot item that increases Gaze of Orobyss gains by +3 upon killing a timeline boss. Note that the needle is consumed on use.
*   **Glyph of Envy:** A rare crafting material that allows you to skip timeline stability farming by instantly resetting a timeline to its boss encounter.

| Item | Rarity | Primary Use |
|---|---|---|
| **World Splitter** | **Unique** | Core weapon for Void Knight and melee builds. |
| **Harbinger Eye** | **Quest/Key** | Required to summon Aberroth at the Altar. |
| **Harbinger's Needle** | **Idol** | Speeding up Corruption gains in Empowered Monoliths. |

## Optimized Farming Strategy

If you are specifically hunting for a 3LP World Splitter or pushing your Forgotten Knights rank, standard monolith running is often inefficient. Follow these steps to optimize your **last epoch harbinger of hatred** farm:

1.  **Use Collapsing Horizons:** This is a new mechanic introduced in the 2026 seasonal updates. By inserting a "Collapsing Horizons" echo into your timeline, you can summon the Harbinger directly without needing to defeat Rahyeh first.
2.  **Corruption Sweet Spot:** For efficient farming, aim for **300 to 500 corruption**. This range provides a significant boost to item rarity and LP chances without making the boss fight excessively long or dangerous.
3.  **The Nemesis System:** If you find a World Splitter with 0 or 1 LP, do not discard it. You can use the Nemesis system to "egg" the item, potentially adding Legendary Potential or powerful affixes through the Weaver's Tree mechanics.
4.  **Woven Echoes:** Purchase Woven Echoes from the Haven of Silk vendor to replenish your stock of special echoes, allowing you to chain-farm Harbingers more effectively.

## Progressing the Forgotten Knights Faction

Each time you slay a unique Harbinger, your rank with the Forgotten Knights increases. There are 10 ranks in total, corresponding to the 10 timelines available in the Monolith of Fate. 

| Rank | Requirement | Major Perk |
|---|---|---|
| **Rank 1** | Kill 1st Harbinger | Unlocks the Shattered Road hub. |
| **Rank 5** | Kill 5th Harbinger | Blessing rolls are rolled twice (take the higher value). |
| **Rank 8** | Kill 8th Harbinger | Increased drop rate for Glyphs of Envy. |
| **Rank 10** | Kill all 10 Harbingers | Unlocks the ability to fight Aberroth using Harbinger Eyes. |

> **💡 Tip:** If you are struggling with a specific Harbinger, check the faction screen to see the "Minimum Required Corruption." If your current corruption is too low, the Harbinger will not spawn, even if you defeat the timeline boss.

## FAQ

**Q: Why isn't the Harbinger of Hatred spawning after I kill Rahyeh?**

A: Check your current corruption level. The Forgotten Knights faction requires a specific minimum corruption for each subsequent Harbinger. If you have already killed several Harbingers in other timelines, the requirement for the **last epoch harbinger of hatred** may have increased to 125, 150, or higher.

**Q: Can I farm the World Splitter in normal (non-empowered) timelines?**

A: While technically possible once you reach the level 90 timelines, the drop rate is significantly lower. It is highly recommended to farm the Harbinger of Hatred in Empowered Monoliths at 300+ corruption for the best results.

**Q: What is the difference between the Harbinger of Hatred and the Harbinger of Defilement?**

A: In the current 2026 build, this is largely a naming discrepancy. The "Harbinger of Hatred" is the official name in the Forgotten Knights quest log, but the boss may display as "Harbinger of Defilement" during the actual combat encounter in the Black Sun timeline. They are the same entity for loot and progression purposes.

**Q: How do I get more Harbinger Eyes?**

A: A Harbinger Eye is guaranteed on the first kill of each of the 10 Harbingers. After you have cleared the initial questline, Harbingers have a high chance to drop additional Eyes when defeated in high-corruption Empowered timelines.
