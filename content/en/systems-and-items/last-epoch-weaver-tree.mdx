The introduction of the Woven Faction has fundamentally changed how players approach the endgame in EHG's hit ARPG. At the heart of this system lies the **last epoch weaver tree**, a massive, malleable talent web that allows you to customize your loot drops, encounter rates, and experience gains. Unlike Item Factions like the Circle of Fortune or Merchant's Guild, the Woven Faction integrates directly into your Monolith of Fate progression, providing a layer of agency that was previously missing from the grind.

Navigating the **last epoch weaver tree** effectively requires a deep understanding of how Memory Amber, Woven Echoes, and imprinting mechanics interact. Whether you are looking to target farm specific Exalted items, push your corruption to new heights, or simply maximize your experience gain to reach level 100, this guide provides the optimal paths and strategies for 2026. By specializing in specific branches of the tree, you can transform your Monolith experience from a random loot hunt into a precision-engineered farming machine.

## How to Unlock the Weaver Tree

Before you can start spending points, you must first gain access to the Haven of Silk. This process begins as soon as you enter the Monolith of Fate. While clearing echoes, you will occasionally encounter **Tombs** or **Cemeteries**. Tombs are small, procedurally generated sub-zones within a regular echo, while Cemeteries are full echoes themselves located on your timeline web.

To unlock the **last epoch weaver tree**, follow these steps:
1. Complete a **Cemetery Echo** on any timeline.
2. Defeat the mini-boss (The First Woven) at the end of the Cemetery.
3. Enter the portal to the **Haven of Silk**.
4. Speak with **Masque** to join the faction and unlock your first set of Weaver Tree points.

Points are earned in two primary ways: by increasing your Woven Faction rank through the collection of Memory Amber and by completing unique **Woven Echoes** for the first time. Each Woven Echo type grants a one-time talent point upon its first successful completion, making exploration of the Woven system mandatory for power gamers.

## Core Mechanics: Memory Amber and Woven Echoes

The Woven system operates on a unique economy. Understanding these currencies is vital for maintaining your tree and upgrading your gear.

| Currency/Item | Primary Source | Usage |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Memory Amber** | Possessed enemies, Tombs, Cemeteries | Respecing the tree, buying Woven Echoes, rerolling Idols |
| **Woven Echoes** | Cemetery rewards, Masque (Vendor) | Placed on the Monolith web to create special challenge maps |
| **Weaver's Touch** | Idol Enchanting Altar | Levels up the exclusive affixes on Enchanted Idols |
| **Crystallized Hearts** | Rift Beasts | High-tier crafting material and quest item |

> **💡 Tip:** Always keep a stack of Memory Amber in your inventory. You will need it to "buy" new Woven Echoes from Masque if you are hunting for specific first-clear talent points.

## The Imprint System: Target Farming Explained

One of the most powerful features of the **last epoch weaver tree** is the Imprint system. There are four primary "tomes" located at the corners of the tree. These allow you to slot in an item from your inventory (without consuming it) to influence the loot table of your echoes.

### Imprint Node Comparison

| Node Name | Location | Item Requirement | Effect |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Commoner's Riches** | Top Right | Any Item | Small chance for enemies to drop a pile of similar items |
| **Sealed Riches** | Bottom Right | Armor or Weapon | Chance for Lost Caches to drop a pile of similar items |
| **Echoing Riches** | Bottom Left | Any Item | Chance for Echo Rewards to drop a pile of similar items |
| **Warlord's Riches** | Top Left | Armor or Weapon | Champions drop items with sealed affixes based on the imprint |

When the tree mentions "similar items," it refers to the base type and, in many cases, the tiers of the affixes. For example, if you imprint a pair of Crystal Swords with Tier 7 Melee Critical Strike Chance, the system will attempt to drop more Crystal Swords with high-tier affixes. This is the single best way to find "double Tier 7" items for legendary crafting at the Temporal Sanctum.

## Strategic Tree Paths for 2026

Depending on your current character level and goals, you should respec your **last epoch weaver tree** to match your needs. The cost of respecing is relatively low, so do not be afraid to pivot.

### 1. The Season Starter Strategy
Early in a season, your goal is to accumulate gold, runes, and your first set of build-defining Exalteds.
*   **Woven Fortune:** Max this first to get more Woven Echoes from Champion kills.
*   **Fury and Fortune:** (Softcore only) Enrages rare enemies but makes them drop significantly more Runes of Havoc and Redemption.
*   **Gatekeeper's Bounty:** Increases the drop rate of Dungeon Keys.
*   **Loot Lizards:** Spec into "Hidden Scales" to get a 30% chance for a lizard to spawn from every cache you open.

### 2. The XP and Favor Rush
If you are racing to level 100 or trying to hit Rank 12 in Circle of Fortune, use the "Exclusion" strategy.
*   **Exclusion Nodes:** Spec into nodes that remove Gold, Arena Keys, Glyphs, and Runes from your echo rewards. This narrows the reward pool, forcing the game to offer Experience Tombs and Favor more frequently.
*   **Tomes of Insight:** Grants a chance for extra experience tomes to drop upon echo completion.
*   **Spiderling Packs:** Adds high-density mob packs to echoes for faster XP gains.

### 3. The Endgame Baseline
Once your build is stable, focus on maximizing the quality of your drops.
*   **Dual Destruction:** Champions now appear in pairs, doubling the chance for sealed affix items and Woven Echo drops.
*   **Exalted Challenger:** Increases the chance for Champions to drop additional Exalted items.
*   **Echoed Legends:** Gives Champions a chance to drop unique items with at least 1 Legendary Potential (LP).
*   **Purge Horizon:** A vital node for 2026 farming. It prevents Shade of Orobyss kills from granting corruption, allowing you to reset your web and keep farming at your "sweet spot" of difficulty without making the mobs too tanky.

## Advanced Woven Echoes and Idol Enchanting

Woven Echoes are not just maps; they are tools. You place them on your Monolith web via the device found at the end of a Cemetery. Some of the most valuable Woven Echoes in 2026 include:
*   **Unclaimed Trove:** Packed with caches; perfect if you have "Sealed Riches" and "Hidden Scales" spec'd in your tree.
*   **Farsight Turtle:** Allows you to reroll the Legendary Potential of a unique item.
*   **The Great Bridge:** A station where you can shatter Set Items to create shards that turn regular items into Set Items.
*   **Rampant Coast:** Essential for Season 3, as it spawns the Rift Beasts required for Crystallized Hearts.

### Idol Enchanting
The Woven Faction also introduced **Idol Enchanting**. By completing a Tomb or Cemetery, you can access an altar to add a "Weaver's Touch" affix to a class-specific idol. These affixes are incredibly powerful and level up as you kill enemies while wearing the idol. You can have up to two enchanted affixes, each reaching a maximum of Tier 7.

> **Warning:** Be careful with the "Warden's Echo" node in Hardcore mode. It can spawn rare enemies from the Soulfire Bastion dungeon inside your echoes. These mobs are notorious for high burst damage and can end a Hardcore run in seconds.

## Target Farming Specific Items

To efficiently farm a specific item, such as a T7 Mana helmet for a Void Knight, you must layer your bonuses. The **last epoch weaver tree** is only one part of the equation. Combine your tree spec with the following:
1.  **Imprints:** Slot the base type you want into the Riches nodes.
2.  **Blessings:** Use the "Stolen Lance" timeline blessing for increased helmet drop rates.
3.  **Woven Echoes:** Use "Vessels of Memory" to reset echoes that previously rewarded the item type you are hunting.
4.  **Prophecies:** If you are in the Circle of Fortune, stack "Exalted Helmet" prophecies.

By aligning these systems, you can ensure that nearly every echo provides multiple opportunities for your desired base type to drop with high-tier affixes.

## FAQ

**Q: Does the Weaver Tree reset when I switch timelines?**

A: No. The **last epoch weaver tree** is global for your character and applies to every Monolith of Fate timeline you enter. You can respec it at any time in the Haven of Silk using Memory Amber.

**Q: Can I use the Weaver Tree alongside the Circle of Fortune?**

A: Yes. The Woven Faction is a "Secondary Faction." You can be a member of the Circle of Fortune (or Merchant's Guild) and the Woven Faction simultaneously. Their bonuses stack, making for incredibly lucrative farming sessions.

**Q: What is the maximum number of points I can get for the tree?**

A: As of 2026, the point cap is determined by your Woven Faction rank and the number of unique Woven Echoes you have completed. Most players will have enough points to comfortably spec into two major branches (e.g., Champions and Loot Lizards) plus all four Imprint nodes.

**Q: Is "Purge Horizon" mandatory for endgame?**

A: It is highly recommended if you have reached a corruption level that is perfect for your build's speed. Killing the Shade of Orobyss normally increases corruption, which might eventually slow down your clear speed. Purge Horizon allows you to reset your web for more rewards without increasing the difficulty further.
