## Crafting Basics: Forging Your Path to Power

Crafting in Last Epoch is deterministic, meaning you have significant control over the outcome compared to other ARPGs. To make the most of your items, you must understand **Forging Potential (FP)**. Every base item drops with a random amount of FP, which acts as a currency for modifications.

### The Crafting Process
1.  **Select Your Base:** Look for exalted items (purple) with at least one Tier 6 or Tier 7 affix that fits your build.
2.  **Use Glyphs of Hope:** Always keep a Glyph of Hope active while crafting. It provides a 25% chance to consume no Forging Potential, allowing for more upgrades.
3.  **Manage Affixes:** You can have two prefixes and two suffixes. Use the Glyph of Chaos to randomly roll an unwanted affix into a different one of the same tier.
4.  **Seal with Despair:** Use a Glyph of Despair for a chance to "seal" an affix into a fifth slot, freeing up one of the main four slots for an additional stat.

## Advanced Target Farming in 2026

Farming in Season 4 requires a strategic approach to the Monolith of Fate. To get the best results, you should balance active farming (targeting specific pieces) with passive farming (collecting shards and idols while clearing).

### Monolith Stability and Corruption
To encounter timeline bosses, you must build Stability by completing echoes. In 2026, pushing Corruption is the primary way to increase item rarity and the number of blessing choices offered after a boss kill.

*   **Stability Loop:** Clear echoes to reach the boss, kill the boss to get a Gaze of Orobyss, then kill the Shade of Orobyss at the edge of the web to significantly boost Corruption.
*   **Corruption Milestones:** At 200 Corruption, you unlock a fifth blessing choice, making it much easier to roll perfect stats for your character.

## Leveraging Item Factions: CoF vs. MG

The choice between the **Circle of Fortune (CoF)** and the **Merchants Guild (MG)** fundamentally changes how you use **last epoch tools**. 

### Circle of Fortune (Self-Found Focus)
If you prefer finding your own gear, CoF provides Prophecies. You can use the observatory to buy Prophecies for specific item types, such as "Exalted Helmets" or "Unique Daggers." Use the tool's database to see which lenses you should equip to maximize the appearance of the Prophecies you need.

### Merchants Guild (Trade Focus)
For those who prefer the Bazaar, the MG allows you to buy exactly what you need. In this case, the tool is best used to price-check items and identify which "Best in Slot" (BiS) exalted affixes are worth selling to fund your own legendary projects.

## Legendary Potential and the Eternity Cache

Farming for Legendaries is the true endgame of 2026. A Unique item can drop with **Legendary Potential (LP)**, ranging from 1 to 4. This allows you to merge the Unique with an Exalted item in the Temporal Sanctum dungeon.

1.  **Find the LP Item:** Higher area levels (level 100 empowered monoliths) increase the chance of dropping items with 2+ LP.
2.  **Prepare the Exalted:** The Exalted item must have four affixes. The stats that transfer to the Unique are chosen based on the LP count.
3.  **The Dungeon Run:** Complete the Temporal Sanctum. At the end, use the Eternity Cache to seal the items together. In Season 4, Tier 2+ dungeons allow for more predictable stat transfers.

| LP Count | Stats Transferred | Success Strategy |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **1 LP** | 1 Random Stat | Ensure all 4 Exalted stats are useful. |
| **2 LP** | 2 Random Stats | Higher chance of hitting your "must-have" prefix. |
| **3 LP** | 3 Random Stats | Almost guaranteed to be a massive power spike. |
| **4 LP** | All 4 Stats | The ultimate chase item; rarity is extremely high. |

## FAQ

**Q: How do I import a build from Last Epoch Tools into the game?**

A: While you cannot directly "import" a character's stats into the game client, you can use the Build Planner to export a Loot Filter. Go to the planner, click the 'Loot Filter' icon on the left, customize your rules, and download the file. Place this in your game's filter folder to see the gear you need highlighted on the ground.

**Q: What is the best way to farm Glyph of Despair in 2026?**

A: Glyphs of Despair are rare drops but can be target-farmed more effectively in the Lightless Arbor dungeon. Spending gold at the Vault of Uncertain Fate at the end of the dungeon frequently offers chests containing rare crafting glyphs and runes.

**Q: Can I use the build planner on my mobile phone?**

A: Yes, the 2026 version of **last epoch tools** features a fully responsive mobile UI. While it is more condensed than the desktop version, you can still allocate skill points and browse the item database on the go.

**Q: Why does the build crawler show "Watch Out" on some guides?**

A: This warning appears if a build was created for a version of the game that is two or more major patches old. Because EHG frequently reworks skill trees (like the recent Lunge or Shaman overhauls), older builds may no longer function as intended. Always check the "Game Version" tag before committing to a build.
